About Us!

We are a bakery just south of Rivendell, near the Mines of Moria.

Founded by Gandalf the Grey!

We have a dine-in, and we serve all over Middle-Earth with express Eagle service!

We serve everyone, including : Hobbits, Elves, Men, Druids, Ents and even Dwarves!
